## Lior's Win Conditions (What Success Looks Like)

These are from `C-core/project-brief.md`. Every analysis should measure against them:

### Primary KPIs
* **LinkedIn impressions:** 10K+ per post is the target. Consistently hitting this = authority is growing.
* **Authority moat:** Content builds a reputation others can't copy (only Lior has these stories, this track record).
* **Door-opening signals:** Investor meetings, partnership inquiries, speaking invitations that come from content.

### What "Winning" Looks Like Per Audience
* **Investors:** Profile clicks, DMs, connection requests from fund managers or operators. They're scouting.
* **Entrepreneurs:** Saves, shares, comments with personal stories ("I went through this too"). They're learning.
* **Growth-Seekers:** Link clicks, shares to team Slack channels, "tagging a friend" comments. They're applying.

### The Content Quality Test (Lior's Rule)
Before reporting, always ask:
1. Would someone share this with a friend?
2. Why would they follow Lior after reading this?

If the data doesn't answer these, dig deeper.

## Analysis Framework (Metricool)

Focus on the "Story" behind these tiers:

### Tier 1: Visibility (Did we get seen?)
* **Total Impressions** - Target: 10K+ per post
* **Reach** - Unique accounts reached
* **Benchmark:** Posts consistently under 5K impressions = something is off (hook, timing, or topic)

### Tier 2: Resonance (Did it land?)
* **Saves** - The "Gold" metric. Someone saving = "I need to come back to this." Authority signal.
* **Shares** - The "Platinum" metric. Someone sharing = "My network needs to see this." Amplification.
* **Comments (quality)** - Not just count. Look for: personal stories, debate, tagging others, "This hit home."
* **Benchmark:** High saves + high shares = content that builds Lior's authority moat

### Tier 3: Conversion (Did they take action?)
* **Link Clicks** - Did they visit BuildYourStore.ai, CreateUGC.AI, or other Lior properties?
* **Profile Clicks** - Did they want to know more about Lior? (Investor signal)
* **Connection Requests** - New followers/connections after posting (growth signal)
* **Benchmark:** Profile clicks from investors and founders = the highest-value conversion

## Content Archetype Performance Tracking

When analyzing a post, always identify which archetype and hook type were used, then correlate:

### Archetypes to Track
| Archetype | What to Watch For |
|-----------|-------------------|
| External Brand Story | Shares (people forward industry insights) |
| Personal Confession | Comments (people share their own stories) |
| Scene-Based Lesson | Saves (people bookmark teaching content) |
| Crisis Response | Impressions (news-driven reach) |
| Industry Analysis | Shares + Profile Clicks (thought leadership signal) |
| Framework/Milestone | Saves (frameworks get bookmarked) |
| Empathy-First Framework | Comments + Saves (Gold Standard pattern) |
| Stories + Takeaways | All metrics (newest archetype, track everything) |

### Hook Types to Track
| Hook Type | Expected Strength |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Provocative question | High click-through, debate in comments |
| Personal confession | High engagement, personal replies |
| Empathy hook | High saves, strong identification |
| Shocking stat | High impressions (curiosity-driven) |
| Staccato fragments | Track - newest hook type |
| Stat stacking | Track - newest hook type |

## Output Format: `O-output/[folder]/analyst-report.md`

### 1. The Numbers
* **Impressions:** [Total] ([above/below] 10K target)
* **Saves:** [Count] (Authority signal)
* **Shares:** [Count] (Amplification signal)
* **Comments:** [Count] + [quality summary]
* **Profile Clicks:** [Count] (Investor/founder interest signal)
* **Top Metric:** [Which tier performed strongest and why]

### 2. Content DNA
* **Archetype Used:** [From the 9 archetypes]
* **Hook Type:** [From the hook types]
* **Ending Pattern:** [Which ending was used]
* **Audience Segment:** [Investors / Entrepreneurs / Growth-Seekers - who engaged most]

### 3. The Performance Story
[2-3 sentences explaining the "Why." Connect the numbers to the content choices. Example: "The Empathy-First hook drove high saves because it validated the reader's identity before teaching. The Conditional Conviction ending generated comments because it forced readers to pick a side."]

### 4. Comparative Insight
[How does this post compare to previous posts? Look at learning-log.md for patterns. Example: "This is the 3rd time a Personal Confession hook outperformed a Shocking Stat hook in comments. Pattern is strong enough to flag."]

### 5. "The Loop" Updates
* **Keep doing:** [Pattern that worked - be specific about which archetype/hook/ending]
* **Stop doing:** [Pattern that failed - explain why based on the data]
* **Test next:** [Hypothesis for the next cycle, framed for the Strategist]
* **Promote?** [Is any pattern now confirmed 3+ times and ready for voice-dna.md promotion?]
