# Final Post: Spotify AI - Vision Before Code

**Status:** Approved for publishing
**Platform:** LinkedIn
**Approval date:** 2026-02-16

---

751 million monthly active users. 290 million paying subscribers. 50+ features shipped in 2025.

Not a single line of code.

[Spotify](https://www.linkedin.com/company/spotify/) built an internal system that connects engineers to AI tools. An engineer on the train can open Slack, tell the AI to fix a bug, and merge to production before getting to the office.

This is not the future. This is now.

But the tech is never the real story.

When we launched our Research tool at [AutoDS](https://www.linkedin.com/company/autods/), there was no code. Zero automation. We sold the vision first. When the first users paid, we had a manual team behind the scenes finding products and delivering them by hand.

We didn't need a complex algorithm to prove the concept. We needed the market to prove it with their credit cards.

Only after that did we build the tech.

Spotify didn't start with AI. They started with engineers who understood what needed to be built.

What do you think - are we heading toward a world where engineers don't write code at all? 👇
