## What Could Be Even Better (Minor Notes)

**Clarity Enhancement:**
- "Then DSM-Tool (the market leader) started beating us" could be even more specific
- Consider: "Then DSM-Tool (the market leader, 3x our size) started eating our market share."
- This adds more visual stakes

**Tension Build:**
- "Two brothers, MBAs, connections, money. I had fear and an internal 'no.'" is good
- Could amplify: "Two brothers with MBAs, connections, funding. I had fear and a voice saying 'you can't compete.'"
- This makes the "internal no" more visceral

**That said:** These are micro-optimizations. The post is publication-ready as-is.
