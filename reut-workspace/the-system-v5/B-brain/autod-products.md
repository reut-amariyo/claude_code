## 1. AutoDS (Flagship Product)

**Website:** autods.com
**Help Center:** help.autods.com

### What It Does
AutoDS is a cloud-based dropshipping automation platform. It connects sellers to 25+ suppliers, automates product imports, pricing updates, order fulfillment, and tracking. It is NOT a supplier - it is management software that sits between sellers and external suppliers.

### Key Stats
- Served 1M+ e-commerce stores
- $150M+ annual GMV processed
- 800M+ products in database, 500M+ from 25+ suppliers
- $15.3M monthly revenue (Oct 2024), up from $7.2M (Dec 2023) - ~112% YoY growth
- 25,000+ active paying subscribers
- 250+ employees at peak (148 at time of acquisition)
- Bootstrapped from $0 (zero external funding)
- Acquired by Fiverr in July 2024 for $92M (remained independently operated)
- Fiverr also acquired Yaballe to strengthen AutoDS's dropshipping position
- 4.9 stars on Shopify App Store (10,630+ reviews)
- 4.9 score on TrustPilot
- 60K+ community members
- 24/7 live chat support, average response under 1 hour
- Founded 2016 by Lior Pozin, Michael Royf, and Ofir Bokobza
- Co-founders: Lior Pozin (CEO), Michael Royf, Ofir Bokobza

### Core Features

**Product Research & Discovery:**
- Winning Products Hub - curated trending products
- Smart Product Finder - AI-powered search across 800M+ products
- Product Database - filter by profitability, trends, niche
- Trends Dashboard - real-time market trends
- Ads Spy Tool - spy on Facebook, Instagram, TikTok ads
- AutoDS Marketplace - curated supplier marketplace
- AutoDS Sourcing Service - finds better supplier deals

**Automation Engine:**
- Automatic price & stock monitoring (updates every 60 minutes)
- Automated order fulfillment (multiple methods)
- Tracking number auto-updates
- Return and refund management
- Smart pricing rules with profit margin protection
- Listings auto-pause when margins drop too low

**Fulfillment Methods:**
1. Fulfilled by AutoDS (FBA) - full automation using AutoDS's own buyer accounts (seller never needs personal buyer accounts or credit cards for purchasing)
2. Auto-Order (full automation) - seller's own buyer accounts
3. Auto-Order (semi-automation) - manual orders + auto tracking
4. Manual orders - complete control
- No order limits on any plan (key differentiator vs competitors)
- AutoDS Warehouse - China-based fulfillment center with exclusive products
- Branded invoicing available (gift card with store logo via Private Suppliers/Warehouse)

**AI Tools:**
- AI product page generation
- AI ad copy creation
- AI image rewriting
- AIWrite tool for listing optimization (titles, descriptions, SEO)
- AI Title Optimizer - rewrites titles for SEO (Etsy, Google, etc.)
- RapidLaunch (AI Store Builder) - build a complete niche-optimized Shopify store in 60 seconds
- AI design editor for Print on Demand

**Print on Demand (built-in, not a separate app):**
- AI design editor for creating custom designs
- Trend-driven catalog: apparel, accessories, mugs, phone cases, footwear
- Catalog updated monthly for seasonal/viral demand
- Integrated across all selling platforms

**Multi-Channel Support:**
- Shopify, eBay, Amazon, Etsy, Wix, WooCommerce, Facebook Marketplace, TikTok Shop

**25+ Integrated Suppliers:**
- AliExpress, Amazon, Walmart, Home Depot, CJ Dropshipping, Banggood, DHgate, Shein, and more

**Additional Tools:**
- Chrome Extension for one-click product importing
- Bulk operations (import, watermark, edit, pricing)
- Sample orders before full launch
- Print-on-Demand custom products
- Virtual Assistant seat management

### Pricing
- $0.99 for 3-day trial
- Base plans: $19.90-$29.00+/month (varies by channel)
- Supports 200-10,000 listings per plan
- Optional add-ons: Orders Processor ($9.90/mo), Product Finding Hub ($14.97/mo), VA seats ($14.97/mo), Mentoring ($39.97/mo)
- Pay-per-use: auto-order credits, AI credits

### Education Ecosystem
- AutoDS Academy - free step-by-step courses
- Mentorship Program - 1:1 guidance from experienced dropshippers
- YouTube channel + blog with regular content
- Webinars and live onboarding sessions
- Community of 60K+ dropshippers

### Target Audience
- Beginning online sellers entering e-commerce
- Dropshipping entrepreneurs scaling operations
- Multi-channel merchants managing multiple stores
- Sellers seeking full automation

### Competitive Advantages (vs Spocket, Zendrop, CJ, DSers)
- Most complete all-in-one solution (research to fulfillment to returns)
- Broadest supplier network (25+ vs competitors' limited lists, e.g. DSers is mostly AliExpress)

- No order limits on any plan (competitors often cap orders)

## 2. ShopShark

**Website:** shopshark.com
**Help Center:** help.shopshark.com

### What It Does
ShopShark is an analytics and research platform built for TikTok Shop, focused on the US market. It helps sellers, brands, creators, and affiliates make data-driven decisions by tracking video performance, product sales, and commission data.

### Brand Voice (from brand kit)
Fast, confident, and clean. Minimal words, high signal. Sharper than AutoDS. Trader mindset, creator-friendly language.

### Core Questions ShopShark Answers
1. What is TikTok pushing right now?
2. Which video sparked the trend?
3. Which products are driving real buyer behavior?
4. Where is the commission potential?

### Key Stats
- 20M+ videos refreshed daily
- Near-real-time data during active streams
- Up to 1 year of historical performance data (Premium)
- Daily data updates

### Core Features

**Videos Page (Popular Videos):**
- TikTok videos ranked by performance
- Engagement metrics: views, likes, comments, shares
- Revenue data: Video GMV (est.), paid commissions (est.)
- Commission percentage tracking (5-20%)
- Weekly GMV growth and daily revenue trend charts
- Filters: product category, revenue range, commission rate, date range
- "Today" filter for last 24-hour trends (Pro/Premium only)

**Video Detail Page:**
- Playable video with creator profile and follower count
- Creator's total GMV
- Time-series trend charts (7, 30, 60, 90 days or custom)
- Selectable metrics: GMV, Commission Rate, Views, Likes, Shares, Comments
- Linked product info with units sold and GMV trends
- Related videos: same product, same creator, similar products

**Products Page (Top Selling Products):**
- Products ranked by sales volume
- 7-day sales trend chart visualization
- Filters: category, status, units sold, GMV, time range
- Category classification and order growth metrics

**Product Detail Page:**
- Rank position across US Top Sales and categories
- Viral index and popularity score (1-100 scale)
- Total units sold, total GMV, affiliate count, shoppable video count
- Estimated launch date, stock levels, price, commission %, seller details
- Customizable time range analysis
- Sales charts with "Gained" and "Cumulative" views
- Related promotional videos with engagement metrics

**Data Capabilities:**
- Revenue at content level (estimated per video and per live session)
- Profit-aware metrics tracking costs and commissions in dollar value
- Time-based performance analysis linking spikes to specific content
- Transparent data confidence (clearly marks estimated vs real data)
