# Prompt: Create a Professional Email

Copy and paste this into your chat (fill in your details first):

---

```
I want to create a new social media post. Here is the topic/goal:
---
Topic: [Insert Topic]
Goal: [e.g., Thought Leadership / Drive Traffic]
---

Please follow the Social Post Workflow:

1. Work as the Strategist (A-agents/strategist-scout.md):
   - Use Google Search to find a current trend or specific data point related to this topic.
   - Create O-output/[folder]/strategist-brief.md with a clear "Angle."

2. Work as the Copywriter (A-agents/copywriter-agent-social.md):
   - Ask me for the target Platform.
   - Once provided, write a draft in copywriter-draft.md based on the Strategist's brief.

3. Work as the Gatekeeper (A-agents/gatekeeper-agent.md):
   - Review the draft for Voice DNA and "Eye-Level" tone.
   - Save your review and, if approved, create final-post.md.

4. Reminder for later: I will provide Metricool stats in 48 hours for the Analyst-Agent to process.

Show me the Strategic Brief and the Copywriter's first draft.
```
