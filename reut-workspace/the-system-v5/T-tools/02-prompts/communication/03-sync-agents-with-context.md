# Prompt: Sync Agents with Your Core Files

Use this after you've filled in your brand core files to make sure all your agents are grounded in YOUR unique voice, business, and ideal customer.

Copy and paste this into your chat:

---

```
I've filled in my brand core files. Now I need all 4 agents to internalize this information.

1. Read my core files: C-core/project-brief.md, C-core/voice-dna.md, and C-core/icp-profile.md.
2. Read all agent files: Copywriter, Gatekeeper, Strategist-Scout, and Analyst-Agent.

3. Confirm for EACH:
   - Does the Strategist understand my "Narrative Hooks"?
   - Does the Copywriter have my latest Voice DNA?
   - Does the Gatekeeper know my "Banned Words"?
   - Does the Analyst know which Metricool KPIs represent a "win" for Lior?

4. Create a summary showing key insights for my brand, voice, and audience.
5. Identify any placeholders in the Strategist or Analyst files.
6. Update M-memory/learning-log.md noting the full 4-agent team is now synced.

Show me the summary and confirm the whole team is ready.
```

---

## Why This Matters

Your agents are only as good as the context they have.

**Before syncing:** Agents write generic content that sounds like everyone else.

**After syncing:** Agents write content that sounds like YOU. Specific to your business, your voice, your audience.

---

## When to Use This

- Right after filling in your core files for the first time
- Whenever you make significant updates to your brand positioning
- When content starts feeling generic or off-brand
- Before starting a big content project
- After running The Loop (when Memory insights get promoted to Core)
