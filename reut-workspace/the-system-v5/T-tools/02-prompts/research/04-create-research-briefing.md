# Prompt: Create a Research Briefing

Copy and paste this into your chat (fill in your details first):

---

```
I need a research briefing. Here's what I'm looking for:

---
Topic: [What do you want to research?]
Focus: [Any specific angle or question?]
Context: [Why do you need this? What decision does it support?]
---

Please follow this process:

1. First, read the workflow in T-tools/03-workflows/research-briefing-workflow.md to understand the process

2. Read my brand core:
   - C-core/project-brief.md
   - C-core/voice-dna.md
   - C-core/icp-profile.md
   - B-brain/research-samples/ (if available)

3. Read the Research Briefing skill: T-tools/01-skills/research-briefing-skill/research-briefing-skill.md

4. Read what we've learned before: M-memory/learning-log.md

5. Check B-brain/INBOX/ for any recently captured material related to this topic

6. Now work as the Researcher (A-agents/researcher-agent.md):
   - Research and compile findings
   - Create a new folder in O-output/ with the next available number
   - Save your briefing as researcher-briefing.md with sources and notes

7. Then work as the Gatekeeper (A-agents/gatekeeper-agent.md):
   - Review the briefing against all standards
   - Save your review as gatekeeper-review.md
   - If approved, create final-briefing.md ready to use
   - If not approved, explain what needs to change

8. Update M-memory/learning-log.md with what you learned from this process

Show me the final result and tell me if it's ready to use or needs revision.
```
