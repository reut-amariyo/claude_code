# Final Post — Team Resilience

**Platform:** LinkedIn
**Published with:** Team photo
**Date:** March 2026

---

No job descriptions have been prepared for my team for the last 2 years.

I've watched them do things you can't train for.

Team members in Israel are jumping back online minutes after missile alerts to keep building. Engineers in Ukraine were shipping code while their cities were under attack.

Nobody asked them to.

I've told every person on the team the same thing: home comes first. Always. Your family, your safety - that's priority.

And still, they show up.

I've hired over 300 people building AutoDS - Automatic Dropshipping Tools. Ran thousands of interviews. There's no question you can ask that reveals this kind of resilience. You only see it when things get really hard.

I didn't get lucky with the hiring process. I got lucky with who these people are.
