# Final Post: NBA All-Star - Start Playing

**Version:** v3 (revised per owner direction - new structure + updated players)

LeBron James missed over 15,000 shots. Kevin Durant missed over 9,200. Deni Avdija missed over 2,200.
Tomorrow is the NBA All-Star game. These guys will be on the court, and nobody in the arena will be thinking about the shots they missed. Only the ones they made.
But when it comes to yourself - you do the opposite. You remember every failed launch, every pitch that didn't land, every idea that went nowhere. You replay the misses on a loop.
Think about it. When was the last time you looked at someone you admire and focused on what didn't work for them? You don't. You see what they built. You see where they are now. Nobody is counting their misses.
So why are you counting yours?
I've missed a lot. Failed products, bad hires, ideas I was sure about that went nowhere. But the things that actually worked - the ones that changed everything - came from the same place. I just kept playing.
Stop keeping score of your misses. Start playing. The people watching aren't counting either.
