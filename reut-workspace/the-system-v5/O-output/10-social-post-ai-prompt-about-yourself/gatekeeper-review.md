### What's Good

1. **The hook works.** "Every AI tool I use knows exactly who I am." One line. Creates instant curiosity. Implies a system. Benefit-driven.

2. **The Reframe is strong.** "The problem was never the tool. It was the input." Quotable. Shareable. Pure Lior.

3. **Rule of 3 framework is clean.** Background, voice rules, audience. Each step is a single dense paragraph. Matches Sample 09 style.

4. **"Not like a robot pretending to be a CEO."** Strong punch line. Self-aware. Funny without trying.

5. **Eye-level throughout.** "Most founders I know" instead of "most founders." "How I'd describe myself to a friend over coffee" is warm and relatable.

6. **The closing pair works.** "Write the prompt once. Use it everywhere." (Direct Command) followed by the either/or question. Clean two-line ending.

---

### What Needs Work (Minor Suggestions)

1. **"Not because I built something fancy."** This is slightly defensive for a sentence that early. Consider cutting it - the next line ("Because about a year ago, I sat down and wrote one prompt") already does the work of showing simplicity.

2. **"It was. The AI doesn't know you. You never introduced yourself."** Three short sentences in a row on separate energy levels. The middle one ("The AI doesn't know you") is the strongest. Consider absorbing "It was." into the preceding paragraph: "...why the output sounds like it was written by a stranger. It was." Then start fresh: "The AI doesn't know you. You never introduced yourself."

3. **"about a year ago"** - works as time anchor, but "a year ago" is slightly tighter. Lior typically drops qualifiers. Per Owner Editing Patterns: tightens multiple qualifiers to one.
