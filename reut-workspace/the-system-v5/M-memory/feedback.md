# Feedback Log

Track feedback on your content. What resonated, what didn't, and what your audience actually said.

---

## The Boundary

This file captures what THE SYSTEM tracks about your audience. It's Memory, not Brain, because your agents generate and organize these insights after you publish.

When audience language is strong enough to become part of your voice, promote it to `C-core/voice-dna.md` or `C-core/icp-profile.md`. That's The Loop.

---

## Why Track Feedback

> "Your audience teaches you how to serve them better."

Feedback is external signal. Unlike the learning-log (internal patterns), this captures:
- Comments on your posts
- DMs and replies
- Engagement patterns
- Direct audience input

---

## How to Use This File

**After publishing:** Log notable feedback
**Weekly:** Review for patterns
**The Loop:** Extract strong insights to learning-log. Promote audience language to C-core/icp-profile.md.

## Patterns to Watch For

**High engagement signals:**
- Topics that get saved/bookmarked
- Posts that generate DMs
- Content that gets shared by others

**Low engagement signals:**
- Posts with no comments
- Content that feels "fine" but forgettable

**Audience language:**
- Phrases they use to describe their problems
- Words that appear in positive comments
- Questions they keep asking
