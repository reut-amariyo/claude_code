# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Who They Are

Lior's content serves three audiences:

### 1. Investors
- Looking for proven operators to back
- Want to see frameworks, data-driven thinking, and track record
- Value battle-tested insights over theory
- Seeking founders who have actually scaled

### 2. Entrepreneurs
- Building ventures from scratch or early stage
- Bootstrapped, self-made founders
- eCommerce entrepreneurs, SaaS builders, startup operators
- Looking for practical, actionable advice from someone who's done it

### 3. Growth-Seekers
- Scaling existing businesses
- Focused on pricing strategy, upselling, growth hacking
- Need frameworks and systems, not motivation quotes
- Managers and operators who want to level up

## What They Want to Achieve

They want to get inspiration and practical tips from someone who made an exit and runs a successful company from zero. They want to learn from both the failures and the successes and apply those lessons in their daily lives.

**Specifically:**
- Investors: find proven operators worth backing
- Entrepreneurs: build from 0 to millions, understand the SaaS journey
- Growth-Seekers: scale revenue through pricing, upselling, branding, and frameworks

## What's Blocking Them

They have fear running them:

- **Fear of starting:** What if I fail? What if I waste everything?
- **Fear of judgment:** What will people think of me?
- **Fear of loss:** Investing time and money and losing it all
- **Information overload:** Flooded with too much advice, too many voices, need focus
- **Need for proof:** They want someone who actually did it, not just talks about it

**What they need:** Someone they can draw inspiration from who is at the place they want to be. Someone who makes entrepreneurship simple and possible, who breaks down the journey from 0 to millions into actionable steps.

## Where They Hang Out

- **LinkedIn** (primary platform for content)
- **Instagram** (lifestyle + behind the scenes)
- **X / Twitter** (real-time takes and networking)
- **TikTok**
- Entrepreneurial communities and masterminds
- Industry conferences and events
- Podcasts
- SaaS and eCommerce communities

## Their Language

**How they think:**
- Direct, bold, no-BS
- Action-oriented ("just start," "just test it")
- Competitive ("if they can do it, I can do it better")
- Results-focused (numbers, metrics, ROI)
- Value frameworks over fluff

**How they want to be spoken to:**
- As peers (CEO sharing, not coach lecturing)
- Zero fluff, high signal
- ROI focused (what's in it for me)
- Real stories, not AI-generated inspiration
- Never arrogant, always actionable
