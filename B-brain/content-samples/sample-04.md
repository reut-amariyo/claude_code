[Shopify](https://www.linkedin.com/company/shopify/) just removed over 10,000 of our reviews.  
Most of them were 5-star.  
  
Years of work, trust, and social proof - gone in one algorithm update.  
  
But when the team showed me the update, my response was simple:  
“Doesn’t matter. Let’s focus on what’s next.”  
  
Here’s the mindset shift that saved us hours of stress:  
When the market changes for everyone, it’s not a setback - it’s a reset.  
An opportunity to rethink, adapt, and win.  
  
If Shopify now favors "high-quality" stores, then let’s use this moment to:  
* Go deeper into verticals and micro-apps  
* Attract even better-fit merchants  
* Get real reviews that reflect our value  
  
And lead again, under the new rules  
Complaining never made anyone a market leader.  
Adapting did.  
Algorithm changes don’t hurt those who keep building.  
They hurt those who stop.
